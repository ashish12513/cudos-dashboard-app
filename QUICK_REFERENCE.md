## 🎮 How to Use Filters

### Step 1: Open Filter Dropdown
Click on any filter dropdown (Payer Accounts, Account Names, etc.)

### Step 2: Select Multiple Options
- **Windows/Linux**: Hold `Ctrl` and click options
- **Mac**: Hold `Cmd` and click options

### Step 3: See Active Filters
Selected filters appear as tags below the filter panel

### Step 4: Remove Individual Filters
Click the **✕** button on any tag to remove it

### Step 5: Reset All Filters
Click **↻ Reset Filters** button to clear everything

## 🎯 Common Tasks

### View Production Only
1. Click **Account Names**
2. Ctrl/Cmd+Click **Production**
3. Done!

### Monitor Multiple Regions
1. Click **Regions**
2. Ctrl/Cmd+Click desired regions
3. Dashboard updates instantly

### Analyze Specific Accounts
1. Click **Payer Accounts**
2. Ctrl/Cmd+Click multiple accounts
3. View combined data

### Clear All Filters
1. Click **↻ Reset Filters**
2. All filters cleared
3. Dashboard shows all data
